Welcome to Sol Plaatje University Student Residence

Sol Plaatje University has 6 student residences in different locations, namely; Mohudi, Tauana, Rathaga, Moroka, Hannetjie and Umnandi.

  • Off-campus: Mohudi and Rathaga
  • Central campus: Moroka
  • South campus: Tauana, Hannetjie and Umnandi

All our off campus residences are a (10-15 minutes) walk away from the central campus making it easy to attend classes and access campus resources.

Residence students at the University identify themselves in many different ways with distinctions that include but are not limited to
race, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, religion, age, ability and life experience.
Out of this diversity, we seek to build a community where we are able to balance our desire to explore individual differences with our
desire to celebrate common bonds, a community that is equally concerned with our rights as individuals and our responsibilities as members.

We understand the importance of having a safe and conducive environment for your studies. Our dedicated staff and numerous amenities ensure that you have everything you need to succeed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students with a secure and supportive living experience during their educational journey. We aim to create a vibrant and diverse community where you can focus on your studies, make lifelong friends, and build memories that will last a lifetime.
