Facilities and Amenities

All SPU-RES:

  • are equipped with 24/7 high-speed WiFi in all communes
  • have single and sharing rooms in communes
  • have game rooms
  • have 24-hour on-site security
  • have common study areas and seminar rooms
  • have fitness center and recreation facilities
  • have laundry rooms on-site
  • have dining halls that use the Meal Management System that requires studentd to book for their meals
  • have turnstiles at every main entrance for secure entry to student rooms

Note: Sol Plaatje University is a smoke and drink free institution!
Smoking in residence buildings is prohibited, however there are designated smoking areas on the grounds.
